Beyond training, we work directly with teams adopting agentic software engineering. Our
advisory is vendor-neutral: we help you make sound choices, keep your stack portable, and
control cost and risk. All engagements are treated as confidential.

## How an Engagement Works

1. **Initial contact.** You reach out by email. If needed, we sign a mutual NDA before any
   substantive discussion.
2. **Scoping call.** A short call to understand the question — your stack, your team, the
   decision at hand, and the depth required.
3. **Deliverable.** From a single workshop or written assessment to an ongoing advisory
   relationship, depending on scope.

## Engagement Formats

- **Workshops and briefings** — focused sessions for engineering teams and leadership.
- **Workflow audits** — reviewing how your teams use agents today and where to improve
  prompting, orchestration, testing, security, and review.
- **Tooling and deployment advice** — choosing models, ecosystems, and architectures;
  monitoring agent performance; reducing cost; and staying sovereign and
  provider-independent.
- **Team and process enablement** — rethinking roles, team composition, and engineering
  process for the AI era.

## Who Might Benefit

- Engineering organisations adopting coding agents at scale
- Teams worried about the security and reviewability of AI-generated code
- Leaders rethinking roles, process, and how to price software in the AI era
- Organisations that want to keep their AI usage cost-controlled and sovereign
