# Agentic Software Engineering

*Training and consulting to help your teams build software with AI agents — effectively, securely, and on your own terms.*
{: .site-subtitle}

Software engineering is being reshaped by coding agents. The question is no longer
*whether* to adopt them, but *how* to do so well: how to prompt and orchestrate agents,
where to keep humans in the loop, how to keep your code secure and reviewable, and how to
deploy all of this affordably and sovereignly. We help teams answer these questions in
practice.

## What We Offer

- **Training** — a modular, hands-on course on agentic software engineering, from first
  principles to production workflows. Mix of lectures and guided practice.
- **Consulting** — advisory and workshops tailored to your stack: auditing your agentic
  workflows, enabling your teams, and guiding tooling and deployment choices.
- **Team enablement** — helping engineering organisations rethink roles, process, and
  team composition for the AI era.

## Why Us

- **Grounded in research and practice.** We track the field closely and translate it into
  concrete, vendor-neutral guidance — not hype.
- **Independence.** We are not tied to a single model provider or tool. We help you choose
  what fits, stay portable, and keep your AI usage sovereign and cost-controlled.
- **Hands-on.** Our material is built around real workflows — MCP, RAGs, tools, bugbots,
  skills and rules, CI integration, and end-to-end use cases.
